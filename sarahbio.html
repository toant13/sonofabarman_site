SARAH COLE

Sarah graduated from the University of Central Florida with a degree in Political Science. Her lifelong interest in film and love of figuring out the world lead her to the art department. She began working on productions three years ago. In that time she has served as production designer on two independent feature films and over a dozen short films. In addition, she has art directed several short films and commercials. She also has experience as the set dresser on an independent feature and two short films.
